Module 02 — Governance

Governance: see how good your AI actually is, before you rely on it.

Governance is the platform capability that gives you the tools to test every assistant you build, producing a clear, plain-language report — so you always know whether to trust it, and why, before it answers real questions for real people.

Built into every assistant. Always on.

What Governance checks, every time

Fairness

Checks whether the assistant treats different people fairly, so you can catch unfair patterns before they cause harm.

Safe language

Checks for harmful, rude, or inappropriate responses across a wide range of test questions.

Traceable answers

Every answer can be traced back to the exact document or record it came from, so you can always check the source.

Consistency

Checks that similar questions get similarly fair answers, so no one is treated differently by accident.

Accuracy

Checks how closely each answer matches your actual documents, so you know it isn't making things up.

Complete answers

Checks that the assistant answers the whole question, not just the easy part — important for policy and legal questions.

What Governance gives you

REPORT

A plain-language Governance report anyone can read and act on — no data scientist required.

DASHBOARD

A simple Ready / Needs review / Not ready view, so you know what to do next.

RECORD

A saved, tamper-proof copy of every Governance check, for your own records.

NEXT STEPS

Clear recommendations tied to each issue found — not just a score.
