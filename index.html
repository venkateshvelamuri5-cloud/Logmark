Module 02 — Governance

Quantify performance.
Deploy with confidence.

Before any AI model influences a hiring decision, a performance review, or a compensation recommendation — leaders need evidence. Logmark's Governance module measures the parameters that matter in talent workflows.

● Built for decision-makers evaluating AI in talent workflows
Bias Detection
Identifies differential treatment across protected characteristics — gender, age, ethnicity, disability — in model responses and ranking outputs.
Toxicity Scoring
Measures the presence of harmful, demeaning, or unprofessional language in model outputs across a library of adversarial prompts.
Auditability
Every decision, ranking, and recommendation is traceable to its source data. SHA-256 signed reports create an immutable chain of evidence.
Statistical Fairness
Applies demographic parity, equalised odds, and calibration tests to model outputs — surfacing disparate impact before it reaches production.
Accuracy & Faithfulness
Measures how closely responses reflect approved knowledge-base content, reducing hallucination risk in high-stakes People Operations contexts.
Completeness
Ensures the model answers the full scope of a query — not just the easiest part. Critical for policy and compliance use cases.
What leaders get
  • A plain-English governance report that non-technical leaders can read and act on without a data scientist in the room
  • RAG-status dashboards (Ready / Review / Remediate) that make deployment decisions straightforward
  • Signed, immutable audit files for legal, compliance, and board-level reporting
  • Recommendations mapped to specific governance gaps — not just scores, but next steps
  • Continuous monitoring after deployment, not just a one-time pre-launch check
